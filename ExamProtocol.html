NCEA Numeracy Exam Protocol

Get ready for exam day with our handy guide!

Rock up 30 mins early with your NSN and ID,dont forgot to pack your pens and calculators and remember to listen to the invigilators.

What is allowed

  • Power banks
  • Rulers
  • Blank paper
  • standard (non-scientific or non-graphing) calculators or approved calculators

What is not allowed

  • phones, notes and watches
  • internet access
  • leaving the Assessment Master platform
  • communicating with others.
